### References and Further Reading

1. Kenneth H. Rosen, "Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications", 7th Edition, McGraw-Hill Education, 2012
2. C. L. Liu, "Elements of Discrete Mathematics", 2nd Edition, McGraw-Hill Education, 1985
3. Ralph P. Grimaldi, "Discrete and Combinatorial Mathematics: An Applied Introduction", 5th Edition, Pearson, 2003.
4. [Lecture Notes on Discrete Mathematics](https://www.cse.iitk.ac.in/users/rmittal/prev_course/s15/notes/complete_notes.pdf), IIT Kanpur
5. [Necklace Problem](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Necklace_problem)
   - Mathematical analysis of necklace arrangements
   - Applications of equivalence relations in combinatorics
6. [Necklace Combinatorics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Necklace_(combinatorics))
   - Detailed study of necklace equivalence classes
   - Connection to group theory and symmetry
7. [Wolfram MathWorld - Equivalence Relation](https://mathworld.wolfram.com/EquivalenceRelation.html)
   - Comprehensive mathematical treatment
   - Examples and visualizations
8. [NIST Digital Library of Mathematical Functions](https://dlmf.nist.gov/)
   - Standard reference for mathematical functions and their properties
   - Section on relations and equivalence classes
